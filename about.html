Our Story

Maithya Café was founded in 2023 with a simple mission: to provide a cozy and welcoming space for our community to enjoy high-quality coffee and delicious homemade pastries. Our founders, inspired by their travels and a passion for baking, wanted to create a place where people could connect and unwind.
